Butter Chicken

Ingredients
  • >800g chicken breast
  • Pinch of chat masala to serve
  • Handful coriander leaves
  • 3cm ginger, finely shredded
  • 1 lime
  • 50g natural youghurt
  • 50g double cream
  • 4 garlic
  • 1 tsp gaarm masala
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 3/4 tsp gorund umin
  • 1 tsp beetsoor powder(optional>)
  • 50g ghee
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 400g tomato passta
  • 1/2 tsp kashmiri chilli powder
  • 45ml double cream

Process
  1. For the first marinade, mix the lime juice, chilli powder and 1 tsp salt flakes in a large bowl. Add chicken pieces, then cover and transfer to the fridge to marinate for 1 hour.
  2. For the second marinade, put all the ingredients into a mini food processor. Blend until smooth, then add to the marinated chicken and stir well to coat. Cover and transfer to the fridge for 4 hours.
  3. Preheat your grill to its hottest setting. Thread the chicken on to lightly oiled metal skewers and grill until lightly charred in places but not completely cooked through, as youâ€™ll finish cooking them in the sauce.
  4. For the sauce, heat the ghee in a saucepan over a medium heat. Add the garlic and ginger and fry for a minute, then stir in the passata and bring to a simmer for 5 minutes. Add all the spices, the coconut, 11/2 tsp salt flakes and 100ml water and simmer for a further 10 minutes.
  5. In a mini food processor, blend the cashew nuts, pumpkin seeds and boiling water to a paste. Stir this into the sauce, followed by the chicken and another 100ml water.
  6. Simmer for 10 minutes, or until chicken is completely cooked through, then stir in the fenugreek, sugar and cream and cook for a further 2 minutes. Sprinkle with chat masala and garnish with fresh coriander and ginger, then serve.
